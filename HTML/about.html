What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical hacking is the practice of deliberately probing systems, networks, and software applications to identify vulnerabilities. By understanding the weaknesses of a system, ethical hackers help organizations strengthen their defenses against malicious attacks.

Skills and Tools of Ethical Hackers

Ethical hackers need a wide range of skills including knowledge of networks, programming, and various hacking tools. Popular tools like Kali Linux, Metasploit, and Wireshark allow ethical hackers to test and secure systems.
